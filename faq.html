Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Studio Chenille

Studio Chenille is based in Ascot, Windsor and Maidenhead, and we serve clients across Berkshire, Surrey, and Greater London, including Windsor, Virginia Water, Sunningdale, Wentworth, Chelsea, Kensington, and Mayfair. We also take on projects throughout the wider UK and internationally for the right commissions.

The cost of interior design varies significantly depending on the scope, scale, and complexity of your project. We offer a range of service levels, from design consultations and concept development to full turnkey project management. We provide a detailed, transparent fee proposal after an initial consultation so you understand exactly what is included. We are happy to discuss budgets openly and can tailor our services to suit a range of investment levels.

Our refined seven-step process covers:

  1. Concept Creation — understanding your vision and lifestyle
  2. Design Development — creating detailed plans, mood boards, and visualisations
  3. Selections — curating furniture, lighting, fabrics, and accessories
  4. Planning & Specifications — producing construction drawings and material specifications
  5. Construction — overseeing all building works
  6. Procurement — managing budgets, orders, and logistics
  7. The Grand Reveal — staging and presenting your completed space

Timelines vary depending on the size and complexity of the project. A single room redesign might take 8 to 12 weeks, while a full home renovation or new build interior could take 6 to 18 months or more. During our initial meeting, we will provide a realistic timeline tailored to your specific project and discuss any time-sensitive requirements you may have.

This can take place at your property, at our studio, or via video call. It is an opportunity for us to get to know each other and for you to see whether Studio Chenille is the right fit for your project.

  • Private residential homes
  • Apartments and penthouses
  • Boutique hotels and hospitality venues
  • Commercial offices and retail spaces
  • Show homes for property developers
  • Leisure and wellness facilities such as spas

Our portfolio includes contemporary, classic, transitional, and eclectic styles.
