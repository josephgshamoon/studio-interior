Our Services

Residential Interior Design

Bespoke interiors for private homes, apartments, and townhouses — designed around the way you live.

Your home should be a reflection of who you are. At Studio Chenille, we specialise in creating residential interiors that combine beauty with practicality, crafting spaces that feel effortlessly luxurious while perfectly suited to everyday life. Whether you are renovating a period property, fitting out a new build, or refreshing a single room, our team brings the same attention to detail and creative rigour to every project.

What We Offer

  • Full interior design for new builds, renovations, and extensions
  • Room-by-room design schemes with mood boards and material palettes
  • Space planning and furniture layout optimisation
  • Bespoke joinery, cabinetry, and built-in furniture design
  • Kitchen and bathroom design, including fixture and fitting selection
  • Curtain, blind, and soft furnishing design and sourcing
  • Lighting design and specification
  • Art and accessory curation and placement
  • Furniture procurement from trusted luxury suppliers
  • Project management and contractor coordination

Our Approach

Every residential project begins with getting to know you. We take the time to understand your lifestyle, tastes, and how you use your home on a daily basis. This insight forms the foundation of a design that is not only visually striking but genuinely comfortable to live in.

From initial concept through to the final styling, we manage every aspect of the design process. We work closely with skilled craftspeople, trusted contractors, and premium suppliers to ensure that every element — from the broadest spatial decisions to the smallest decorative touches — meets the highest standards.

Whether your style leans towards contemporary elegance, classic refinement, or something entirely unique, we create interiors that feel authentically yours. Our aim is always the same: to deliver a home that you are proud of and that brings you joy for years to come.
