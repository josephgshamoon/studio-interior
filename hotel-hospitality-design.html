Our Services

Hotel Interior Design

Designing memorable guest experiences for boutique hotels, guest houses, and hospitality venues.

In hospitality, the interior is inseparable from the guest experience. At Studio Chenille, we design hotel and hospitality interiors that tell a story, set a mood, and leave a lasting impression. From intimate guest houses to boutique hotels, we create environments where every detail contributes to an atmosphere of comfort, style, and distinction.

What We Offer

  • Full interior design for boutique hotels and guest houses
  • Guest room design with a focus on comfort and functionality
  • Reception, lobby, and communal area design
  • Restaurant, bar, and dining room interiors
  • Spa, wellness, and leisure facility design
  • Brand-aligned interior concepts and colour palettes
  • Commercial-grade material and finish specification
  • Bespoke furniture design and FF&E procurement
  • Lighting design for ambience and functionality
  • Full project management from concept to handover

Our Approach

Hospitality design requires a careful balance between visual impact and operational practicality. We begin every project by understanding the venue's brand identity, target guest profile, and commercial objectives. This ensures the design not only looks exceptional but also supports efficient day-to-day operations.

We select materials and furnishings that are built to withstand the demands of commercial use while maintaining the visual warmth and refinement your guests expect. Every specification is considered with durability, maintenance, and guest comfort in mind.

From the moment a guest enters the lobby to the time they settle into their room, the interior should create a seamless, immersive experience. We design with this journey in mind, ensuring that each space transitions naturally into the next, building a cohesive narrative throughout the property.
