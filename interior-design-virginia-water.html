Interior Design in Virginia Water

One of the most exclusive addresses in the South East, Virginia Water is synonymous with luxury living. The area, including the prestigious Wentworth Estate, features some of the finest residential properties in the country, set within beautifully landscaped grounds.

Designing for Virginia Water homes requires an understanding of high-end finishes, bespoke craftsmanship, and the expectations of discerning clients. We bring this expertise to every project, creating interiors that match the calibre of these exceptional properties.

Studio Chenille brings a considered, client-focused approach to every Virginia Water project. We listen carefully to your brief, develop designs that exceed expectations, and manage the entire process with professionalism and care.
