Our Services

Commercial Interior Design in Ascot & Across the UK

Creating inspiring workspaces, retail environments, and corporate interiors that reflect your brand.

The spaces where people work, shop, and do business have a direct impact on performance, perception, and wellbeing. At Studio Chenille, we design commercial interiors that go beyond aesthetics — creating environments that communicate your brand values, support your team, and make a lasting impression on clients and visitors.

What We Offer

  • Office design and workplace interiors
  • Retail shop and showroom fit-outs
  • Corporate reception and boardroom design
  • Co-working and flexible workspace design
  • Space planning and layout optimisation
  • Brand-aligned interior concepts and wayfinding
  • Acoustic planning and partition specification
  • Commercial furniture sourcing and procurement
  • Lighting design for productivity and ambience
  • Full project management and contractor coordination

Our Approach

We approach every commercial project by first understanding how the space needs to function. Who will use it? How do teams collaborate? What impression should visitors and clients take away? These questions shape every design decision we make, from spatial planning to material selection.

A well-designed commercial interior should feel purposeful. We create environments that balance open collaboration areas with private meeting rooms and quiet focus zones, ensuring that the layout supports the way your organisation actually works. We also pay careful attention to factors such as natural light, acoustics, and ergonomics — the elements that make a genuine difference to daily comfort and productivity.

We understand that commercial projects often need to work around existing operations. Our team develops detailed phased plans to minimise disruption, and we manage the entire process from initial design through to final installation and sign-off.
