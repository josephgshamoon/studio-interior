### Hero journey (homepage)

A 320vh wrapper (`#heroJourney`) pins a sticky 100svh hero; scroll progress drives a lerp-smoothed timeline. Two auto-selected engines:

- **zoom mode** (default): layered zoom-through of three portfolio photos
- **video mode**: canvas frame-sequence scrub; activates when `images/hero/manifest.json` has `frames > 0`. Frames are generated by `tools/hero-video-to-frames.sh` from a Seedance 2.0 video â€” the generation recipe is `HIGGSFIELD-HERO-RECIPE.md`. Delete the manifest values back to the placeholder (`frames: 0`) to revert to zoom mode.

Falls back to a classic static hero when JS is absent, `prefers-reduced-motion` is set, or `overflow-x: clip` is unsupported.

**Traps:**
- `body` must use `overflow-x: clip`, never `hidden` â€” `hidden` makes body a scroll container and silently breaks the sticky pinning (style.css declares `hidden` then `clip` deliberately, as a fallback chain).
- The header's transparentâ†’solid switch in `main.js` is coupled to `#heroJourney`'s height; changing the wrapper height or hero structure affects it.
