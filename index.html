About Us

Interior Design,
Refined

Studio Chenille is a team of dedicated and dynamic professionals — designers and managers who collaborate to create luxurious and innovative interiors tailored to each client's unique vision.

Based in Ascot, we specialise in residential and hotel projects across the United Kingdom and internationally. Our portfolio spans interior remodelling, new construction, and bespoke finishing work across private residences, boutique hotels, corporate offices, show homes, and leisure facilities.

Every project we undertake is guided by a commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of how spaces shape the way people live, work, and feel.

How We Work

Our 7-Step Design Process

Every project follows our refined seven-step methodology, ensuring a seamless journey from initial concept to the final reveal.

01

Concept Creation

We begin by understanding your vision, lifestyle, and aspirations. Through detailed consultations, we establish the spatial flow, mood, and aesthetic direction for your project.

02

Design Development

Our designers develop detailed plans incorporating architectural features, materials, fixtures, and colour palettes that bring the concept to life through mood boards and visualisations.

03

Selections

We curate bespoke furniture, lighting, fabrics, and accessories — sourcing from trusted artisans and luxury suppliers to ensure every piece is perfect for your space.

04

Planning & Specifications

Comprehensive documentation including detailed floor plans, elevations, material specifications, and construction drawings to ensure every detail is meticulously planned.

05

Construction

We oversee all construction and installation work, coordinating with trusted contractors to ensure the highest standards of craftsmanship and quality throughout.

06

Procurement

Full project management including budgeting, accounting, and logistics. We handle every procurement detail so you can focus on the excitement of your new space taking shape.

07

The Grand Reveal

The moment you've been waiting for. We stage every element to perfection, capture professional photographs, and present your beautifully transformed space. Welcome home.
