Who We Are

About Studio Chenille

A team of dedicated professionals creating luxurious and innovative interiors from our studio in Ascot, United Kingdom.

Our Story

Based in Ascot,
Designed for You

Studio Chenille is a team of dedicated and dynamic professionals — designers and managers who collaborate to create luxurious and innovative interiors tailored to each client's unique vision.

Operating from our studio in Ascot, Windsor and Maidenhead, we work across the United Kingdom and internationally. Our projects encompass interior remodelling, new construction, and bespoke finishing work — from private residences and boutique hotels to corporate offices, show homes, and leisure facilities.

We believe that great design begins with listening. Every client brings a unique set of aspirations, and our role is to translate those aspirations into spaces that feel both extraordinary and entirely personal.

Our Philosophy

Design with Purpose

Every project we undertake is guided by a commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of how spaces shape the way people live, work, and feel.

Attention to Detail

We approach every element of a project with meticulous care — from the overarching spatial concept down to the finishing touches that make a space feel complete and considered.

Client Collaboration

Our design process is built on close partnership with our clients. We listen carefully to understand your vision, preferences, and lifestyle, ensuring the final result is authentically yours.

Bespoke Solutions

No two projects are the same. We create tailored design solutions that respond to the unique character of each space, each brief, and each client we work with.
