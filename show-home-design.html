Our Services

Show Home Interior Design

Aspirational interiors for property developments and new builds that help buyers fall in love with a space.

A beautifully designed show home transforms an empty property into an aspirational lifestyle. At Studio Chenille, we create show home interiors that speak directly to your target buyer, helping them visualise the full potential of each room and form an emotional connection with the space. Our designs are carefully considered to appeal to the right market while showcasing the property's best features.

What We Offer

  • Full show home interior design from concept to installation
  • Target buyer profiling and design strategy
  • Space planning to maximise the perceived size and flow of each room
  • Colour scheme development and material selection
  • Furniture, soft furnishing, and accessory sourcing
  • Bespoke window treatments and dressing
  • Art selection and decorative styling
  • Kitchen and bathroom accessory dressing
  • Coordination with developers and build teams
  • Installation, styling, and professional photography coordination

Our Approach

Show home design is a distinct discipline within interior design. It is not about creating a lived-in home — it is about creating a carefully curated experience that helps buyers imagine their best life within the space. Every choice, from furniture scale to colour temperature, is made with the target buyer in mind.

We work closely with property developers to understand the target market, price point, and unique selling points of each development. This informs a design strategy that highlights the property's strengths, addresses any potential concerns, and positions the development favourably against competitors.

Our team manages the entire process, from early-stage concept development through to the final styling on installation day. We coordinate closely with build teams to ensure timelines align, and we handle all procurement and logistics so that the show home is ready to impress from the moment the doors open.
